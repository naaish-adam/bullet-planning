Calendar

Create and edit events, manage tasks by creating to-do list for the day, and set reminders. Sync your google, outlook and apple calendar to see all your events in one app.

Note taking

Create digital notebooks, capture your thoughts and ideas safely and share it with your colleagues / family and friends. Insert PDFs and images to your notes and create a paperless notes you can access anytime from anywhere.

Daily trackers, Monthly and Yearly logs

Helping your keep a chronological log of life.

Daily logs: Events/Tasks/Reminders, Expense tracker, Habit tracker, Meal tracker, Mood tracker.

Monthly logs: Habit tracker, mood tracker, spending trackers, interests, monthly review.

Yearly logs: Vision board, Goals Bucket list, Future log, Travelogue.

About

A productivity mobile application built on the concept of bullet journaling. The application is developed in two versions, to support Apple IOS and Android Operating System. The application is available for download for free and is available in the English language. The app concept is build around providing users with a guide to increase motivation and productivity and track their daily lives.
