FEATURES

DAILY LOGS

The Daily log is designed for day-today use!

Tasks / Events / Reminder

Add all the things you need to get done today. All the events you need to attend, and any reminders you need to note. Tick on the box when a task is completed. To add the events, choose the date/time and location of the event and allow to get a reminder before 30 minutes. You can even add an event for a future date by choosing the event date and get a reminder at your convenience. Reminders work like your alarm. Add daily reminders and get notified to get your tasks done.

Expense Tracker

Add your expenses for the day by selecting the category and adding details with the amount spent. This hassle-free technique boils budgeting down to the only thing many users want to know; how much they have left for spending in the current month. It crunches the numbers to show how much money is available for the rest of the month while providing the opportunity to practice conscious spending.

Habit Tracker

At the beginning of each month, start by adding the list of habits you want to keep a track of. Mark them with a simple click after completion of each habit, from the daily overview dashboard. You can also see the streak of the habits completed and reflect on how well you were able to complete the habit.

Meal Tracker

Log your meals on a daily basis, by adding to the categories breakfast, lunch, dinner and snacks from the dashboard. Simply type what you eat, add calories (optional) and take a picture of your meal. Keeping track of your diet could never get simpler than this.

Mood Tracker

Tracking your mood provides valuable information for monitoring your mental health. At the end of each day select your mood by clicking the mood tracker dashboard from the daily bar. This will help you focus on your attitude, so you don’t let it control you so easily, and it’s helpful to see your emotional patterns over time to practice mindfulness and understand your emotions.

Notes

Add your daily notes with one click and save it to the respective notebook created. For example, if you are working on a project, you can create a notebook only to collect notes related to that project. This way, any notes (meeting minutes, important notes, pdfs, pictures) can be attached to the project notebook which allows you to find all the related documents in one place.

Notebook

Create your notebooks for easy access to a digital notebook stack. Different notebooks for different categories can be created and related notes can be added to keep a track of your work and to give the note-taking process an organized gist.

MONTHLY LOGS

Get a birds eye view of what happened during the month!

Overview: Serves as a way to log notable snippets that happened during the month.

Overview

This feature allows you to give an overview of your productivity over the month. All the tasks, events and reminders will be reflected on the monthly overview. This gives you a chance to see the monthly hassle over the month and gives a reflection on how much you have achieved the targeted tasks.

Expense Tracker

This feature allows you to give an overview of your productivity over the month. All the tasks, events and reminders will be reflected on the monthly overview. This gives you a chance to see the monthly hassle over the month and gives a reflection on how much you have achieved the targeted tasks.

Habit Tracker

Tracking your daily habits and keeping track of those can be a messy job. This component allows you to give a bird’s eye view of your habits and how much of it you were able to do it. You might be surprised at how rewarding it is to see yourself working towards your dreams with simple few clicks.

Meal Tracker

At the end of the month you can see at a glance with pictures what you have eaten throughout the month. You have the option to edit and delete the existing information as part of the convenience of the meal tracking process.

Mood Tracker

Tracking your mood everyday becomes a significant part of life as you can reflect on your overall mood of the month from the monthly mood tracker feature. This component will provide a mood chart illustrating your everyday mood.

Interests

This section has categories to keep a log of all the books you have read or are planning to read throughout the month, all the movies and series you watch in the month, new places you have discovered and new people you have met in the month. Keep the track of all of these in one place.

Monthly Review

This section is all about self reflection. it enables you to process and make meaning of all of the great (and not so great) learning and working experiences you have had in the month. It has a separate section to write the month in a few words, what went well, what did not go well, what to improve, success of the month, lessons learnt and what's for next month. You can also put up a photo as the highlight of the month.

YEARLY LOGS

Set yourself up for a successful year!

Future Log

This section serves as your time machine to list down any notes for future months. Add future events, goals, long-term tasks, birthdays, travel plans and major holidays every month, which allows you to reflect on the outlines of the future you are actively working towards.

Bucket List

Create a thoughtful list of things you want to achieve here. A bucket list forces you to look at the life you are living and the life you would like to say you’ve had. So go ahead and create a list of all the things you want to do in the year.

Goals for the year

Add your goals for the year under the four main categories, Career, Personal, Travel, and Education. This section allows you to list the action plans for each goal, creating a clear pathway to align your focus towards reaching the goals. At the end of the year, you can reflect on the accomplishments as you can see the achieved goals in one section.

Vision board

Create digital versions of your yearly vision boards with important texts, quotes and messages that inspire to boost the desires and aspirations of your thoughts. Add pictures and links to give a creative representation of your compelling. You can also separate your vision boards into different categories to make it easier for reaching your biggest and most important life goals.

Travelogue

Keep a track of all your travel details including trip start date to end date, your favorite memories, and most importantly add all your trip pictures to create a separate gallery of the trip. The trip gallery is shared with other users and allows the trip participants to add their collection of the pictures which makes it easier to find all the details of the trip in one place.
